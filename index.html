NFC Card Lifecycle

All special cards become trackable assets. NFC makes the game controllable: verification, redemption, transfer, fraud prevention, and KPI tracking.

1

Unclaimed

Card pulled from pack but not registered.

2

Claimed

Customer or girl registers ownership.

3

Transferred

Gifted, traded, or moved to a girl's KPI account.

4

Redeemed

Discount, gift, action, or mission benefit used by staff.

5

Collection Counted

Bling Girls Card contributes to KPI or full set progress.

6

Archived

Expired or redeemed cards remain collectible but inactive.

These rules protect the girls, the venue, and the commercial system.

Consent-first action cardsFlirty or physical action cards are requests, never rights. Girl refusal must be allowed.
✓
Staff redemption onlyDiscount, gift, and action cards should be redeemed by staff to control abuse.
✓
No stackingDiscounts cannot stack unless a specific combo card explicitly permits it.
✓
Expiry by card typeGirls Cards remain permanent; utility cards expire by season or stated date.

Commercial structure

Easy to sell at the table.

The product keeps the customer offer simple while hiding enough depth underneath for collecting, swapping, staff selling, repeat visits, and rare-card hype.

Full box hero product

A real opening moment, not just a coupon pack.

The full box is designed to feel like a premium trading-card display set: ten sealed packs, one hundred cards, visible NFC value, and enough theatre for staff to upsell at the table.

  • 10 sealed packs per box
  • 100 cards per opening
  • At least one Gold card guaranteed

Rarity ladder

Nightlife language, not game jargon.

The tiers use terms that feel natural inside LUXE: White, Silver, Gold, Champagne, and Black Diamond.

NFC ownership layer

Special cards become trackable assets.

White cards stay simple. Every Special card gets NFC so LUXE can verify cards, register owners, handle transfers, redeem benefits, and track KPI progress.

01Tap to verifyShow season, serial, rarity, card type, and authenticity.
02Claim ownerCustomer or girl registers card ownership in the LUXE card backend.
03TransferCustomer gives a card to a girl, girls exchange cards, or collectors trade cards.
04Redeem / KPIStaff redeems utility cards. Bling Girls Cards update collection progress.
Staff KPI mechanic

Girls sell the game, not only the box.

Each girl has one ultra-limited Bling Girls Card. If there are 20 girls and only three Bling copies per girl, the full collection has a hard maximum of three possible winners in Season 1.

5Bling cardsSmall bonus tier. Keeps KPI reachable early in the season.
10Bling cardsMedium tier. Encourages repeat selling and exchange behavior.
15Bling cardsHigh tier. Creates serious chase and table conversation.
20Full setJackpot tier. All twenty girls collected, with maximum three winners by print control.
Sample card content

Cards customers can understand instantly.

The wording should stay clear, premium, and staff-controlled. Anything flirty is an invitation, not a right, and should always have an alternative reward.

Gold Discount

Champagne 20

20% off selected champagne tonight or on the next visit. Not stackable. Staff redemption only.

Champagne Action

Photo Moment

Invite one cast member for one approved photo moment. Consent required. Alternative reward available.

Silver Gift

Vodka Set

Free vodka set with qualifying table spend. One redemption per table per night.

Mission

Three Nights

Visit three nights within the season and unlock a member stamp or limited mission reward.

Bling Girl

Season Cast Card

One of three NFC copies for a specific girl. Used for collection, trading, and staff KPI progress.

Black Diamond

VIP Ceremony

Ultra-rare season card. Unlocks a controlled VIP celebration package or headline reward.

Operating guardrails

Designed to protect the venue.

The system should feel fun and premium while staying controlled, consent-based, and operationally manageable.

Consent firstAction cards are requests. Girls may refuse and staff can substitute another benefit.
Staff redemptionDiscounts, gifts, and actions are validated by staff through NFC to prevent abuse.
No stackingRewards cannot stack unless a specific combo card explicitly permits it.
Expiry by typeGirls Cards remain permanent; reward and action cards expire by season or stated date.
Ownership historyNFC transfer records prevent fake winners and fake KPI completion.
Legal reviewJapan reward mechanics, adult venue wording, and physical interaction rules need review before launch.
